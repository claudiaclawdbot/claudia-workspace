### What FIDGET-SPINNER Does

**Instead of letting that liquidity rot, you deposit it with us.**

Here's what happens:

1. **You Deposit Your Dead LP Tokens**
   - Find any LP token in your wallet (e.g., "DOGE/WETH LP" from a dead memecoin)
   - Deposit it into the FIDGET-SPINNER vault
   - We permanently take custody ‚Äî you can't get the original LP back

2. **You Receive an "F-Token" Receipt**
   - We mint you a new token called something like "F-DOGE"
   - This represents your contribution to that specific "dead pool class"
   - It's your proof of recycling

3. **You Stake Your F-Token to Earn $SPIN**
   - Lock your F-Token in our staking contract
   - Earn $SPIN rewards over time
   - $SPIN is the protocol's governance + reward token

4. **When You Want Out, You Burn**
   - Unstake anytime
   - The protocol "burns" (destroys) some $SPIN
   - You keep your F-Token as a collectible/receipt
   - The original dead LP stays in our treasury forever
